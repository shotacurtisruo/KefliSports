Kefli Sports Club

Empowering Youth Through Sports

Kefli Sports Club is a student-led organization at UC Davis dedicated to providing free after-school and weekend sports programs for school children in the greater Davis community. We believe in instilling core principles and values like teamwork, effort, kindness, and character development through mentorship and sports participation for grades K-12.
