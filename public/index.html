Kefli Sports Club

Empowering Youth Through Sports

Kefli Sports Club is a student-led organization at UC Davis dedicated to providing free after-school and weekend sports programs for school children in the greater Davis community. We believe in instilling core principles and values like teamwork, effort, kindness, and character development through mentorship and sports participation for grades K-12.

Rules for Kefli Sports games:

1. KSC believes that no child should ever be picked last which is why drafts at events are hereby banned and teams will be assigned randomly by the event manager with a best effort to keep fair teams and an even number of players on each team.
2. Each player is entitled to even playing time regardless of skill level (ideally the entire game).
3. Each player will be allowed to play the position they want at some point during the game, but not for the entire game unless no other player would like to play that certain position.
4. Keeping score is optional, but for tournament style games there can be winners and losers. KSC teaches children how to win and lose gracefully.
5. Any disputes that happen in game are up to the discretion of the head manager of the event, who has the final call.
6. One board member must have first aid equipment on hand at all times in the case of injuries or medical emergencies.
7. Celebrations with your team are encouraged (such as hitting the Super man or cranking that Soulja Boy) but taunting the other team is strictly prohibited.
8. Most importantly, HAVE FUN!!!
